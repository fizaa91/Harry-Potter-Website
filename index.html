Read Harry Potter Books

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

Harry discovers he's a wizard on his 11th birthday and begins his magical education at Hogwarts. He encounters new friends and uncovers the secret of the Philosopher's Stone.

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

Harry returns to Hogwarts for his second year, where a series of mysterious attacks lead him to discover the dark secrets hidden within the Chamber of Secrets.

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

In his third year, Harry learns about Sirius Black, a dangerous prisoner who has escaped from Azkaban and is believed to be after him. Harry uncovers shocking truths about his past.

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire

Harry is unexpectedly entered into the Triwizard Tournament, a dangerous magical competition. As he faces deadly tasks, dark forces rise, and the return of Voldemort looms.

Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix

Harry faces a growing threat as Voldemort gains power. He joins the Order of the Phoenix and learns about a secret prophecy that could determine his fate.

Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince

In his sixth year, Harry uncovers Voldemort’s dark past and learns about the Horcruxes, objects that hold pieces of the Dark Lord's soul. Meanwhile, danger and betrayal are closer than ever.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows

The final battle against Voldemort begins as Harry, Ron, and Hermione set out on a quest to destroy the Horcruxes. The fate of the wizarding world hangs in the balance.

Watch Harry Potter Movies

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone
(2001)
Harry Potter discovers his magical heritage on his 11th birthday, entering the enchanting world of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Alongside new friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, he uncovers dark secrets and confronts the dark wizard Voldemort.
Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets
(2002)
In his second year at Hogwarts, Harry faces a series of mysterious attacks that petrify students. With the help of his friends, he investigates the legend of the Chamber of Secrets and battles a dark entity that threatens the school.
Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban
(2004)
Harry learns that Sirius Black, a dangerous prisoner, has escaped from Azkaban and is believed to be after him. As Harry delves into Sirius's past, he uncovers truths about his own family and the betrayal that led to his parents' deaths.
Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire
(2005)
Harry is unexpectedly entered into the dangerous Triwizard Tournament, a magical competition between schools. As he faces perilous challenges, he uncovers a plot by Voldemort to return to power and confronts the dark wizard’s growing influence.
Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix
(2005)
Returning for his fifth year at Hogwarts, Harry grapples with the Ministry’s denial of Voldemort’s return and forms Dumbledore's Army to prepare his peers for the coming battle. The fight against dark forces intensifies as secrets are revealed.
Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince
(2009)
As Voldemort's power grows, Harry and Dumbledore investigate the dark wizard’s past to find the key to his defeat. Meanwhile, Harry discovers a mysterious book that reveals crucial information and confronts deep personal challenges.
Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows – Part 1
2010
Harry, Ron, and Hermione leave Hogwarts to hunt down Voldemort's Horcruxes, objects containing pieces of his soul. As they travel across the country, they uncover hidden truths and prepare for the final confrontation with the dark wizard.
Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows – Part 2
2011
The epic conclusion unfolds with Harry and his allies facing their final battle against Voldemort. The fight reaches Hogwarts, where long-hidden secrets come to light, and Harry must confront his destiny to save the wizarding world.
